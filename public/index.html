Solar Eclipse Story

By ABC Brisbane Interactive Updated Tue 8 Aug 2017, 10:47 AM AEST

The US solar eclipse is almost here.

Sometimes the moon gets in the way of the sun and that creates a shadow on the earth. This is what is known as a solar ecipse. If you are unlucky enough to miss the one over the United States in 2017 don't worry because they happen in Australia too.
